Welcome!

Welcome to cinescout, a web app that allows you to learn more about almost any widely-released film or person in the world of cinema.

Things you can do:

  • Read a short synopis of the film, as well as a review summary from The New York Times.
  • Discover the filmography of your favourite actress/actor or crew member.
  • Create an account to add movies to a personal list for later reference.
  • Browse through a list of critically-acclaimed films from The Criterion Collection.
